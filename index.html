About me

Hello! I am a PhD student in Mathematics at Università degli Studi di Torino.

The focus of my research is mathematical logic, with a particular interest in computability, and descriptive set theory. I am working under the supervision of Raphaël Carroy and Paul Shafer.

This site is a work in progress, hopefully in the (near) future there will be interesting things to see here.
